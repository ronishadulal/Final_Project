The Ama Foundation

The Ama Foundation is a grassroots, US-based 501(c) (3) non-profit organization that supports Ama Ghar, a permanent care home in Nepal for at-risk homeless children who have been abandoned, trafficked or have lost their home and family to poverty, drug/alcohol abuse or illness. We are dedicated to providing these children with a loving home, an excellent education, health care and the confidence to become productive, self-supporting adults.

To donate to Ama Ghar every time you shop, go to https://www.goodshop.com/nonprofit/ama-foundation and select us as your chosen organization. Thank You!

Working Together For a Bright Future

Ama Ghar is a permanent care home where children are treated as individuals and raised in a loving family environment. Their talents and interests are encouraged, they receive individual mental and physical health care and we take a very active interest in their education, from attending parent-teacher conferences to providing private tutors when necessary. The Tragedy of
Child Trafficking
in Nepal

Families in high poverty areas of Nepal are often tricked by traffickers into giving up their children with promises of a better life. Instead, these children are put to work doing slave labor in factories, fields, brothels or other abusive situations. They often suffer from malnutrition and mental and physical illness and are trapped in the cycle of poverty.

You can help break this cycle.

By supporting Ama Foundation, you are helping us to rescue these children and give them a childhood, an education and the opportunity for a happy, healthy future.
